Privacy Policy

Your data belongs to you.

Local-First Architecture

Ronnin Pro adopts a strict Local-First architecture. All your fuel logs, trip data, components, and profile settings remain exclusively on your physical device via IndexedDB. We do not maintain centralized servers.

Location Processing

Location permissions are strictly used to calculate live trip speed, GPX tracking, and to request environmental info from third-party APIs (Open-Meteo, OpenStreetMap). These requests are anonymous and unlinked to your identity.

Document Generation

When generating PDF reports or JSON backups, the file compiling is processed entirely locally within your browser using JavaScript. No personal information is ever transmitted for rendering.
